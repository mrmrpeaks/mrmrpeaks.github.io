Songs
Atlantic 45 - The Finest Disco, Funk & RnB

The Atlantic 45 song list covers some of the very best funk, disco, soul and RnB compositions the 20th century has to offer. We have perfected a varied selection of tracks and the quality of music here is a testament to the musiciancship and dedication present in this band.

If there are any particular tracks or genres you would like us to favour when putting together a set just contact us for a chat and we can work our sets around you and your audience.

Additionally, with both a female and male singer Atlantic 45 are an extremely flexible band, if you have a favourite song which you would like us to play please request this when booking the band, if we feel it fits our style we will endeavour to learn it for your event. This is in addition to our optional service of learning a song for your first dance.
