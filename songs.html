Atlantic 45 - The Finest Disco, Funk & RnB

The Atlantic 45 song list features some of the very best funk, disco, soul and RnB compositions the 20th century has to offer.

If there are any particular tracks or genres you would like us to favour when putting together a set just contact us for a chat and we can work our sets around you and your audience.

Additionally, if you have a favorite song or would like a live first dance at your wedding, get in touch and we will look into preparing this for your event.
